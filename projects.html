List of the projects I've worked on:

Rainbow Snake:
A simple recreation of the classic arcade game snake. The head of the snake will always remain white, while it's body and the food you hunt down are constantly changing color every time the snake moves. Hence the name "Rainbow Snake".

Virus:
A simple virus that replicates itself on key press. This virus doesn't do much harm besides slow down your computer after too many windows open up. The virus will start every time you turn on your computer. In order to get rid of the virus, you must type in "password" while focused on the window, or press the escape key. (You could also go to task manager to get rid of the virus).
This project was done simply out of curiosity, and has never been deployed on anyone. Never the less, if you feel the need to unleash it upon yourself, you can click the link to download it from my github page.
